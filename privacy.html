Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2, 2025

No Data Collection

Open 3D Tagger does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The app operates entirely on your device with no server component, no analytics, and no third-party services.

NFC Access

The app uses your iPhone's NFC hardware to read and write data on physical NFC tags attached to 3D printer filament spools. NFC data is processed locally on your device and is never sent anywhere.

NFC access requires your explicit permission the first time the app initiates a scan. You can revoke this permission at any time in iOS Settings.

No Network Connections

Open 3D Tagger does not make any network requests. It does not connect to the internet, does not use cloud storage, and does not communicate with any external servers or APIs.

No Third-Party Services

The app does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics frameworks, advertising networks, or tracking services.

Data on NFC Tags

When you write data to an NFC tag, that data is stored physically on the tag itself. The app does not retain a copy of what was written. Anyone with an NFC reader can read data from a tag you have written.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
